How to Make Homemade Alfredo Sauce

Description

With a few simple steps you can prepare some creamy alfredo sauce right at home! Below you will find everything that you will need.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Directions:

  1. Melt your butter and then stir in cream and simmer
  2. Whisk in the chesse and garlic until heated through and smooth
  3. Stir in the parsley
